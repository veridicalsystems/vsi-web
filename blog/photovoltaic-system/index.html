Photovoltaic System

In the fall of 2012 we completed a major photovoltaic system installation.Â  In addition to giving us tree-hugging bragging rights and potentially being a reasonable financial investement this system also provides backup power to keep the business computing infrastructure running through the frequent power outages in this area. As of May 2013 (six months of operation) this system has generated 8.5 megawatt-hours and seamlessly overridden multiple utility outages that would have meant hours of lost work each time.
